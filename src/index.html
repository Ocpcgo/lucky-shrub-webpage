About Us

Lucky Shrub is a medium-sized garden design firm based in Tuscon, Arizona. We specialize in creating beautiful and functional gardens, offering services in garden design, maintenance, and landscaping.

Founded by a husband and wife team, Jason and Maria, Lucky Shrub combines their shared passion for plants and expertise in creating stunning outdoor spaces. Jason serves as the garden architect, overseeing all designs and managing our team of skilled landscapers. Maria handles the marketing and management of our small plant nursery, where we offer a wide variety of indoor and outdoor plants. At Lucky Shrub, our mission is to transform any space into an oasis you can be proud of. Whether you need a complete garden overhaul or regular maintenance, we are dedicated to delivering exceptional results that reflect your personal style and enhance your surroundings.
